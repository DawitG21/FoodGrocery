About us

Best-in-Class B2B Crowdsourced Last-Mile Delivery for emerging markets

Inibla is an on-demand courier service that purchases, picks up, and delivers products ordered through its mobile app. It aspires to be a multi-category lifestyle app with food delivery being the most popular offering

Hassle-Free Cash Collection Process

Because +80% of deliveries in the Addis Ababa region require cash-on-delivery, we built a Digital Wallet for every business and driver dealing with us. The result is a very organized payment process which includes paying back the cash we collected for you in weekly payment cycles through a list of supported banks with no additional transferring fees.
